Welcome To BKG Electro Fab

Founded in the year 2016, Bkg Electro Fab has carved a remarkable niche in market and known for its quality work. We are working as a sole proprietorship based firm. The head office of our company is situated at Kolkata, West Bengal. We are foremost manufacturer and trader of Metal Flange, Metal Bolt, Metal Angle and Industrial Gasket. These products are examined by our team before the final dispatch.
