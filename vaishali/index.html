About Us

Established in the year 1997, Vaishali Poles is one of the distinguished manufacturer immersed in the arena of presenting to our customers highly reliable Swaged Pole, High Mast Pole, Bend Pipe, Junction Box, etc. The personnel delivering these services are selected from some of the capable personnel present in the industry. Broadly cherished owing to its reliability, these offered services are highly acclaimed.
We make sure to fulfill the commitment of quality to diverse industrial regions, and we do this by presenting innovative product designs that meet the business goals of our esteemed clientele. Since the inception, we are striving to sustain the highest levels of integrity to synchronize soaring product needs with best quality pipes and fittings. We make sure to sustain ethical and transparent business deals and partner with credible vendor base to source premium grades of raw materials.
Since we have incepted our base in this industry area, we are working underneath the direction of Mr. Rahul Kumar Sharma.
