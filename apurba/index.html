About Us

Kicking off the curtain raiser back in 2008, a business with a vision to serve the clients, we envisioned a future where top-notch brands would unleash a new world of possibilities for buyers and sellers of every magnitude. Today, with obstinate commitment to excellence and innovation, we have become a channel partner of several Industrial Electrical and Power Transmission Products. Apurva Enterprises since then, has grown a good place in business of supplying goods to Government & Private Sectors. We can say with pride that we are contributing and playing a good role with supply of International brands of Electrical & Mechanical goods, impelling businesses for well over a decade. Out-growing from being just a channel partner, Apurva Enterprises has also transitioned into a place of procurement specialists helping customers to diminish supply chain cost and amplify efficiency. It is one platform that brings together the finest of Brands from across the globe under one giant canopy for displaying and sourcing products & services pragmatically. We keep our focus intact, working as a One Stop Shop; providing complete solution to our client's under One roof.
